"QA for beginners, 2022"

Mentor: Maria Brodskaya | 2022

Testing is one of the most important steps in the software development process. quality control is essential for the release of any of the applications that we use every day.

Checking software health, analyzing requirements, finding defects and helping to fix them as soon as possible are the main tasks of testers. in most projects, testers are involved in the development process at all stages.
